Our Policies

Below are the policies related to dog purchase, shipping, and medical care.

1. Purchase Policy

  • All sales are final. Ensure you choose the right breed for your needs.
  • We provide a certificate of authenticity for purebred dogs.
  • Customers must be at least 18 years old to purchase a dog.

2. Shipping Policy

  • Shipping is available only within specified regions. Check availability at checkout.
  • All dogs are shipped with proper care and in accordance with animal welfare standards.
  • Shipping costs vary depending on location and breed size.

3. Medical Policy

  • All dogs come with an initial health checkup certificate.
  • Vaccination and deworming are completed before delivery.
  • Customers are advised to follow up with their local vet for routine checkups.
